About Yaa

Yaa Assoh Akoi is the lead therapist at Rapha Physio Care. She was born and raised in Accra and obtained a Bachelor of Science in Physiotherapy from the University of Ghana School of Allied Health. A certified physiotherapist with the Ghana Physiotherapy Association, Yaa has been treating patients in Accra since 2011. She's worked with clients from 3 years old to over 60 years, treating a wide range of illnesses. She can be reached at yaa@raphaphysiocare.com or 0242748512
